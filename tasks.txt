Task 2 (Impact vs. Connectivity): Does a higher volume of collaboration correlate with a higher individual academic impact (h-index/citations)?

Goal: Allow the user to visually compare a researcher's position in the network (centrality) against their actual academic output (encoded as size).

Task 3 (Thematic Clusters): How do distinct research areas (fingerprints/topics) drive collaboration groups?

Goal: Discover if specific groups of collaborators are tightly knit because they share specific dominant topics (e.g., "Silicon Photonics" or "Systems-on-a-Chip").

Task 4 (Ego-Network Details): What is the specific collaboration history and organizational footprint of a selected faculty member?

Goal: Provide a detailed, isolated view of a single researcher, showing exactly who they work with, where those people are located, and how many shared publications they have.
